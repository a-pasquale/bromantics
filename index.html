About The Band

The Bromantics explode onto stage as a high-voltage, six-piece powerhouse transforming venues into electric time machines. With infectious rhythms and angular guitar hooks, they deliver sets that splice the DNA of icons like Talking Heads, The Clash, and The Cure with the raw energy of R.E.M. and U2.

Seamlessly blending dance-floor anthems with moody post-punk atmospherics, The Bromantics perform both masterful covers and original compositions. Whether you're a Gen-X veteran or new convert, their shows prove these sounds aren't just of their time – they're timeless.
