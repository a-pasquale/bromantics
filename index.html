About the Band

The Bromantics explode onto stage as a high-voltage, six-piece powerhouse that transforms any venue into an electric time machine. With infectious rhythms and angular guitar hooks, these musicians deliver blistering sets that splice the DNA of icons like Talking Heads, The Clash, and The Cure with the raw energy of R.E.M., The Replacements, and U2.

From the dance-floor euphoria of The Cars and The English Beat to the moody atmospherics of Jesus and Mary Chain, The Bromantics perform both masterful covers and original compositions that draw from influences spanning barroom blues to classic new wave.
