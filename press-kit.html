High-voltage new wave energy from Western Massachusetts

Based in Western Massachusetts, The Bromantics have been electrifying venues across New England with their authentic recreation of the new wave and post-punk sound that defined a generation. Their commitment to both musical precision and explosive live performance has earned them a devoted following and consistent bookings at premier venues throughout the region.

Band Bio

The Bromantics explode onto stage as a high-voltage, six-piece powerhouse transforming venues into electric time machines. With infectious rhythms and angular guitar hooks, they deliver sets that splice the DNA of icons like Talking Heads, The Clash, and The Cure with the raw energy of R.E.M. and U2.

Seamlessly blending dance-floor anthems with moody post-punk atmospherics, The Bromantics perform both masterful covers and original compositions. Whether you're a Gen-X veteran or new convert, their shows prove these sounds aren't just of their time – they're timeless.

Technical Rider

Venue Requirements

  • Stage Size: Minimum 16' x 12' preferred
  • Power: Standard 110V outlets (6-8 required)
  • Setup Time: 45-60 minutes
  • Sound Check: 15-20 minutes
  • Performance Duration: 1-3 hours (1-2 sets)

Equipment

  • Instruments: 3 Vocals, 3 Electric Guitars, 1 Acoustic Guitar, Bass, Keyboard, Synth, Drums
  • Amplification: Band provides all instruments and amps (3 guitar amps, 1 bass amp). Venue to provide direct box for acoustic guitar
  • PA System: Venue to provide house PA and sound engineer or band can provide own PA
  • Monitors: 4 stage monitors preferred (including drum monitor)
